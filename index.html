About Me

I'm currently expanding my skills focusing on building dynamic, full-stack web applications. I’m always looking for new challenges to enhance my coding abilities and work on real-world projects.

My passion for coding began when I was 13, when my IT teacher helped me develop my first webpage using just HTML and CSS. This early exposure sparked my interest in technology and set me on the path to becoming a web developer. Over the years, my journey into tech has given me a unique perspective on problem-solving and communication, especially as I learned English as a second language. I’m passionate about continuous learning, and I'm excited to take on new challenges in full-stack development.

Fun Fact: I was born in Poland, and English is my second language! Learning to code while mastering a second language has been challenging, but it’s also shaped my approach to problem-solving and creative thinking.
